### 📊 Financial & Tax Benefits
- **Input Tax Credit (ITC)** — Claim back 18% GST paid on all business expenses:

## GST Compliance Notes

- GST must be shown on all **tax invoices** issued to B2B clients
- For B2C (fans, general public), GST invoice is optional but can be issued on request
- File GSTR-1 (outward supplies) monthly/quarterly
- File GSTR-3B (monthly summary) by 20th of the following month
- Maintain all invoices & bills for 6 years (required for ITC audit)
- **Threshold**: No need to charge GST if annual turnover stays below ₹20 Lakh (service sector). Above ₹20L, mandatory to charge & remit.
