# How to Update an Existing Page (The "Class 10 Baby" Guide)

Don't worry about the coding, the terminal, or the complex stuff. I (the AI) and Google Stitch will do all the heavy lifting!

Here is your super simple, step-by-step guide to updating an existing page (like our Home Page). 

---

## 🛑 Before We Start
*   Make sure Google Stitch is open on your browser.
*   Make sure I (Antigravity AI) am chatting with you.

---

## Step 1: The AI Gives You the "Magic Words"
1. Tell me what you want to change. (Example: "I want a new Hero Section on the Home Page.")
2. I will write a highly detailed paragraph for you. These are the "Magic Words" (the prompt).
3. **Your Job:** Just copy those magic words.

## Step 2: You Give the Magic Words & "Reference Link" to Stitch
1. Go to Google Stitch.
2. **💡 PRO TIP (Using Your Existing Website):** Look at the text box where you type. See the little `+` button on the bottom left? 
   - Click the **`+`** button.
   - Click **"Website URL"**.
   - Type in your live website link: `https://www.bongbari.com`
   - *This tells Stitch: "Look at my real website and use those exact words and structure, but make it look like the new design I am asking for!"*
3. Now, in the main text box, paste the "Magic Words" I gave you.
4. Press the button to generate the design!

## Step 3: You Choose the Best Picture
1. Google Stitch will draw a beautiful picture of what the website will look like.
2. **Your Job:** Look at the picture. 
   - If you don't like it, tell Stitch to change things (Example: "Make the button red instead of blue").
   - If you **LOVE** it, go to Step 4.

## Step 4: You Tell Me to "Make It Real"
1. In Google Stitch, every design has a specific **Project ID** or a link (URL) at the top.
2. **Your Job:** Copy that Stitch Project ID (or URL).
3. Come back here to Antigravity and paste it to me. 
4. Say: "Here is the Stitch Project ID: [Paste it here]. Please build this!"

## Step 5: I Do All the Hard Work
1. **Your Job:** Sit back and relax! ☕
2. I will automatically talk to Stitch.
3. I will steal the exact code for that picture using the `react-components` tool.
4. I will carefully place it into the `BongBariComedy` website folders without breaking anything.

## Step 6: You View the Amazing Result!
1. Go to your local website link: `http://localhost:5173`
2. **Your Job:** Smile because your new, beautiful design is live with zero coding errors!
